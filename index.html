    HOW TO PLAY

    GOAL

    Dodge oncoming traffic, stay in your lane, and survive as long as you can. Your score rises the longer you drive — boost with nitro for bonus points, but watch your fuel!

    KEYBOARD

    • ◄► or AD — change lanes
    • ▲ or W — nitro boost (hold)
    • ▼ or S — brake (hold)
    • Space — start / restart / resume
    • P / Esc — pause / resume
    • C — switch camera (close / far view)

    MOBILE / TOUCH

    • Swipe left / right — change lanes (or browse vehicles on the start screen)
    • Hold left half of screen — brake
    • Hold right half of screen — nitro
    • Tap — start, pause, or resume

    TIPS

    • Pick a vehicle before you start — each handles differently.
    • Nitro drains fuel; it refills slowly when idle. Empty tank locks nitro until fuel recovers.
    • Traffic gets faster over time — stay alert!
    • Day turns to night as you play — headlights kick in after dark.
    • Use the SFX, music, and camera buttons (top-right) to adjust sound and view.
    • Top 3 scores are saved on this device.
